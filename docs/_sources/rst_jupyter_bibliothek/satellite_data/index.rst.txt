In addition to climate data from the **Copernicus Climate Data Store (CDS)**, **satellite data** plays a crucial role in **climate impact assessment and environmental monitoring**. Missions such as **Sentinel-2** and **Landsat** provide valuable insights that help cities and municipalities **analyze land use changes, assess vegetation health, and identify urban heat islands**.

In this section, we provide a collection of **practical Jupyter Notebooks** that demonstrate how to **retrieve, process, and analyze** Sentinel-2 and Landsat data. These examples offer valuable guidance on utilizing satellite-based Earth observation to support **sustainable and climate-resilient urban development**.
